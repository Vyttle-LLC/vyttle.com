Stockpot

Your recipes. Your cloud. No servers.

Stockpot is a privacy-first recipe manager that stores everything in your iCloud. Import recipes from the web, scan cookbook pages, and share collections with family — all without creating an account or trusting your data to another server.

Features

  • Import recipes from any URL
  • Scan cookbook pages with OCR
  • iCloud sync across all your devices
  • Share recipe collections with family
  • Meal planning and grocery lists
  • Cook mode with large text and timers

Your data stays yours. We don't collect any personal information. Recipes sync via your iCloud account — we never see them.
