Stockpot Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

The Short Version

We don't collect any of your data. Your recipes are stored in your personal iCloud account. We never see them, and we have no way to access them.

Data Storage

Stockpot uses Apple's iCloud service to store and sync your recipes. This data is stored in your personal iCloud account and is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

We do not operate any servers that store your recipes or personal information.

Data Collection

Stockpot does not collect:

  • Personal information
  • Usage analytics
  • Crash reports (unless you opt in via Apple)
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Location data

Recipe Import

When you import a recipe from a URL, the app fetches that webpage directly from your device. We do not route this traffic through our servers or log which URLs you visit.

Sharing

If you share a recipe collection with others, that sharing is handled by Apple's CloudKit Sharing feature. The shared data remains in iCloud and is not processed by us.

Third-Party Services

Stockpot does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Stockpot does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app does not require an account and does not collect personal information from any user.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact us at support@vyttle.com
