Common Questions

Where are my recipes stored?

Your recipes are stored in your personal iCloud account. They sync automatically across all your Apple devices signed into the same iCloud account.

How do I import a recipe from a website?

Tap the + button and choose "Import from URL." Paste the recipe link and Stockpot will automatically extract the recipe details. Most major recipe sites are supported.

Can I share recipes with family members?

Yes! You can share individual collections with other iCloud users. They'll be able to view and add recipes to the shared collection.

Why can't I see my recipes on a new device?

Make sure you're signed into the same iCloud account and that iCloud Drive is enabled. Recipes may take a few minutes to sync on first launch.

How do I scan a recipe from a cookbook?

Tap the + button and choose "Scan Recipe." Point your camera at the recipe page and Stockpot will use text recognition to extract the ingredients and instructions.
