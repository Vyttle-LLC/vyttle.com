Common Questions

What ratio should I use?

It depends on your taste and brew method! A good starting point is 1:16 (one part coffee to 16 parts water). Slide toward 1:13 for stronger coffee or 1:18 for lighter brews.

What do the strength labels mean?

The labels (Light, Medium, Strong) are guides based on common preferences. The actual ratio number is shown alongside so you can learn what works for you.

Why does my coffee taste weak/strong?

Beyond ratio, factors like grind size, water temperature, and brew time all affect extraction. As a general rule: finer grinds and hotter water extract more, coarser grinds and cooler water extract less.

What's the difference between pour-over and drip?
