Brew Dose Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

The Short Version

We don't collect any of your data. Brew Dose is a simple calculator that runs entirely on your device. Nothing leaves your phone.

Data Storage

Your preferences (like favorite brew methods and ratio settings) are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage. If you enable iCloud, these preferences may sync across your devices via your personal iCloud account.

We do not operate any servers that store your data.

Data Collection

Brew Dose does not collect:

  • Personal information
  • Usage analytics
  • Crash reports (unless you opt in via Apple)
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Location data

Third-Party Services

Brew Dose does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

In-App Purchases

Brew Dose offers an optional tip jar. These transactions are processed by Apple and are subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not receive any personal information from these transactions.

Children's Privacy

Brew Dose does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app does not require an account and does not collect personal information from any user.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact us at support@vyttle.com
