Tidy Contacts Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

The Short Version

We don't collect any of your data. Tidy Contacts processes your contacts entirely on your device. We never see your contact information, and we have no way to access it.

Contact Data

Tidy Contacts requires access to your contacts to function. This access is used solely to scan, analyze, and modify contacts at your request. All processing happens on your device.

Your contact data is never transmitted to any server. We do not operate servers that could receive this data.

Data Collection

Tidy Contacts does not collect:

  • Personal information
  • Contact data
  • Usage analytics
  • Crash reports (unless you opt in via Apple)
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Location data

Backup Files

When you export a backup of your contacts, the .vcf file is created locally on your device. Where you share or store that file is entirely up to youâ€”we never see or access it.

Third-Party Services

Tidy Contacts does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

In-App Purchases

If you purchase the full version of Tidy Contacts, that transaction is processed by Apple and is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not receive any personal information from these transactions.

Children's Privacy

Tidy Contacts does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app does not require an account and does not collect personal information from any user.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact
